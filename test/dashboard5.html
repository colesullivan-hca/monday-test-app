Core Statutory Parameters

Lodging Cap Check ($350)Memo Req
Any night exceeding $350 (including tax) requires a signed pre-approval exception memo routed to Carolee Graham and Jeanette Lujan-Thornburg.
Same-Day Travel Matrix≥ 11 Hours
Reimbursements are valid for same-day travel only if total time elapsed between departure and arrival expands past 11 hours.
Transportation EscalationRide-Share 1st
Ride services are the default primary method. Rental vehicles require alternative memo justifications and explicit CFO sign-off.
SHARE System ControlsPre-Verify
Travelers must be verified as active suppliers with valid IDs in SHARE prior to booking, or a W-9 must be instantly dispatched.
